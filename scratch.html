History of The Masters

The Masters, held annually at Augusta National Golf Club in Georgia, is steeped in tradition and excellence. Since 1934, it has stood as one of the most iconic tournaments in golf history.

  1. Famous Masters Moments:
    • Tiger Woods' first win in 1997
    • Jack Nicklaus' historic comeback in 1986
    • Bubba Watson's hook shot in 2012
  2. Masters Traditions:
    • Green Jacket Ceremony
    • Champions Dinner
    • Amen Corner

About Augusta National

Augusta National Golf Club is famed for its stunning landscaping, pristine fairways, and legendary layout. It is a sacred ground for golf enthusiasts around the world.
